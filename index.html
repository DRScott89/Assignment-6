Biography of Daashawn Scott

Background

Hello! Welcome to my page. My name is Daashawn Scott and I am currently 33 years old residing in the Charlotte Metro area of North Carolina. I was born on September 13, 1989 in a small city in Virginia. During my formative years I attended public schooling in Prince George, VA. I graduated form Prince George High School in 2007 and continued my education at Norfolk State University in Norfolk, VA. In 2011, I completed my undergraduate studies and recieved my B.A in Sociology. After a few years of pursuing my career in criminal justice, I received my first job offer as a Correctional Officer at Sussexx II State Prison in 2013. The following year, I was accepted into the University of Baltimore and shortly after accepted and internship with the Bureau of Prisons as a Student Research Trainee. In 2016, I completed my M.S in Criminal Justice and was promoted to Records Management Analyst. In 2020 I received another promotion as a Correctional Programs Supervisor and relocated to Yazoo City, MS. After one year in this position, I relocated back to the East Coast and have most recently been working with Electrolux as a Warranty Coordinator.

Interests

My interests include jetskiing, spending time with my family, traveling with my friends, cooking, gardening/yardwork and playing with my dog Bella. I have a love for completing DIY home projects, a few of which are included in my gallery section. I am a former tenor saxophone player of 12 years. I have a love various genres of music. I am also a member of Kappa Alpha Psi Fraternity, Inc. I have recently become interested in computer programing and software development, which I what I plan to acheive through Coding Temple.
